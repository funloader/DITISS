### A. Information Technology (Amendment) Act, 2008 (ITAA 2008)

#### Background

* Original Act: **IT Act, 2000**
* Amendment enacted: **2008**
* Effective from: **27 October 2009**
* Reason: Rise in cybercrime, data breaches, national security threats

#### Key Objectives

* Legal recognition of electronic records
* Define & penalize cybercrimes
* Introduce **data protection & corporate liability**
* Regulate intermediaries

---

## 4. Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)

---

### Evolution Timeline (MCQ Favorite)

| Year     | Event                                             |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| 2017     | Puttaswamy Judgment ‚Äì Privacy = Fundamental Right |
| 2018     | Srikrishna Committee Report                       |
| 2019     | PDP Bill introduced                               |
| 2021     | JPC review                                        |
| Aug 2022 | PDP Bill withdrawn                                |
| Nov 2022 | Draft DPDP Bill                                   |
| Aug 2023 | DPDP Act enacted                                  |

---

### Six Pillars / Principles of DPDP

1. Lawful & Fair Processing
2. Purpose Limitation
3. Data Minimization
4. Accuracy
5. Storage Limitation
6. Accountability & Security

---

### Rights of Data Principals

* Right to **Access**
* Right to **Correction**
* Right to **Erasure**
* Right to **Grievance Redressal**
* Right to **Withdraw Consent**

---

### Obligations of Data Fiduciaries

* Provide clear **notice**
* Obtain **free, informed, unambiguous consent**
* Ensure **data security**
* **Report data breaches**
* Conduct **DPIA** (for significant fiduciaries)

---

### Consent Framework

* Consent Manager introduced
* Consent must be:

  * Free
  * Specific
  * Informed
  * Unambiguous
* Easy withdrawal mandatory

---

### Legitimate Uses (Without Consent)

* Compliance with law
* Medical emergencies
* Employment purposes
* Voluntary disclosures

---

### Penalties (High-Value MCQ Area)

* Maximum penalty: **‚Çπ250 Crore**
* Focus on **penalties**, not compensation
* Makes data protection a **Board-level issue**

## 5. ITAA 2008 vs DPDP Act 2023 (Comparison)

| ----------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Nature      | Amendment               | Comprehensive Act         |
| Coverage    | Sensitive Personal Data | All Digital Personal Data |
| Enforcement | Courts                  | Data Protection Board     |
| Liability   | Compensation            | Monetary penalties        |
