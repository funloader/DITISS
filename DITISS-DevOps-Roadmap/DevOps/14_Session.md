### ðŸ“– **2. Key Definitions**

* **Agile:** Iterative and incremental approach to software development that emphasizes **flexibility, customer feedback, and continuous improvement**.
* **Scrum:** Agile framework based on **time-boxed iterations (sprints)**.
* **Kanban:** Agile method focused on **continuous flow of work**.
* **Lean:** Philosophy aimed at **eliminating waste** and maximizing value.
* **DevOps:** Culture combining **development + operations** for faster delivery.
